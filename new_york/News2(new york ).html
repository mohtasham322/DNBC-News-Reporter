Teenager killed and five others injured in crash on Long Island, New York


A teenager was killed and five others were injured in a crash on Long Island last weekend. The two-vehicle collision occurred at Port Jefferson Station, at the intersection of Nesconset Highway and Woodhull Avenue, at around 9.30pm on Saturday, March 11. Suffolk County Police Department said an 18-year-old was driving a 2019 Honda Accord eastbound on Nesconset Highway when he attempted to turn left onto Woodhull Avenue and was hit in the intersection by a 2022 Ford Bronco traveling westbound. A 16-year-old who was a backseat passenger of the Honda was killed in the crash. Police said the 19-year-old driver of the Ford, the driver of the Honda and three teenage passengers in the Honda were all hospitalized. Their injuries are believed to be non-life-threatening, police added. Detectives are investigating the circumstances surrounding the crash.
