Watch: Passengers on Aer Lingus flight to New York treated to live seisiún on board

Long-haul flights can often be a dull affair, but one journey across the Atlantic was made infinitely better this weekend when a group of talented musicians treated passengers to an authentic seisiún on board. It all unfolded on an Aer Lingus flight on Saturday morning from Dublin to New York JFK. A video showing passengers enjoying the music was shared on Reddit, where it’s racked up more than 40,000 upvotes, or likes.

The original poster of the video, Reddit user @dmartin716, said that the music session started up after the plane had landed while people were waiting to get off. In the clip, passengers can be seen standing up and gathering around a group of trad musicians, who were in full flow on the fiddle, accordion and tin whistle. One passenger can be heard joking: “We wasted a lot of time on the flight!” - suggesting that he would’ve liked the music to kick off earlier as some in-flight entertainment
