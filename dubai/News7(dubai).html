UAE Central Bank wins award for new Dh1,000 banknote

The UAE has won an international award for its new Dh1,000 banknote.

The Best New Banknote award was given to the UAE Central Bank at the Banknote and ID Document of the Year Awards in Abu Dhabi.

The UAE’s Dh1,000 banknote won for its unique design, technical characteristics and innovative security features.

The UAE Central Bank unveiled the new Dh1,000 currency note last year as part of the country’s 51st National Day celebrations.

The banknote became available in Central Bank branches and ATMs in the first half of 2023, while the current Dh1,000 note will continue to be in circulation.
