Maharashtra Budget: Eknath Shinde govt focuses on women's safety

In its first budget, the Eknath Shinde government has focused on women's safety and empowerment. The government has announced a 50% discount on women's state transport. Presenting the budget, Finance Minister Devendra Fadanvis announced that the coverage of the Mahatma Phule Jan Arogya scheme has been raised from Rs 1.5 lakh to Rs 5 lakh.
