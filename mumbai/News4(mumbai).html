Man arrested with banned e-cigarettes worth Rs 66 lakh

A man has been arrested in Mumbai after 3,000 banned e-cigarettes worth Rs 66 lakh were recovered from his car. The man was carrying 3,000 e-cigarettes of different flavours. E-cigarettes have been banned due to health concerns.
